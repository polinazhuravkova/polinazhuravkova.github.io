About :

Polina Zhuravkova is a visionary Set Designer and Production Designer renowned for her exquisite creative contributions to the world of film and stage.
With a keen eye for detail and a profound understanding of visual storytelling, Polina crafts immersive environments that breathe life into narratives. Her journey into the realm of design began with an innate passion for transforming spaces into compelling backdrops that evoke emotions and amplify storytelling. Polina's dedication to her craft is evident in her ability to seamlessly blend aesthetics, functionality, and storytelling, creating captivating worlds that transcend the boundaries of imagination. Having collaborated on a spectrum of projects, from independent films to large-scale productions, Polina Zhuravkova brings a wealth of experience, versatility, and innovation to every endeavor. Her meticulous attention to historical accuracy, cultural nuances, and thematic coherence ensures that each set design serves as a character in its own right, complementing and enhancing the overall narrative. Polina's expertise extends beyond mere set decoration; she orchestrates entire visual landscapes, balancing practicality with artistic brilliance. Her designs, whether evoking a specific era, building futuristic worlds, or conjuring fantastical realms, reflect a profound understanding of the director's vision while infusing her unique touch of creativity. Her collaborative spirit and commitment to excellence make her an invaluable asset to any production team. Polina Zhuravkova continues to push the boundaries of design, bringing stories to life through her unparalleled artistry and dedication to crafting immersive experiences that captivate audiences worldwide.
